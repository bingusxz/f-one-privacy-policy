Privacy Policy for MF One

Introduction

Mandaue Foam values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using the MF One app. This app is intended exclusively for employees of Mandaue Foam.

1. Eligibility

The MF One app is exclusively for use by employees of Mandaue Foam. By using this app, you confirm that you are an employee of Mandaue Foam. Unauthorized use by non-employees is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

Employee Information: When you register and use the MF One app, we may collect information such as your name, employee ID, department, and contact details.

Authentication Data: To ensure secure access, we may collect authentication data such as usernames and passwords.

b. Usage Data

App Usage: We collect information on how you interact with the app, including access times, features used, and pages viewed.

Device Information: We collect data about the device you use to access the app, including device type, operating system, and IP address.

3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide and Improve the Service: We use your information to operate and enhance the MF One app, ensuring it meets the needs of Mandaue Foam employees.

Security and Compliance: We use your data to protect against unauthorized access, and to comply with legal obligations.

Communication: We may use your contact information to send important updates and information related to the app.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Access to the MF One app is restricted to authorized employees only, and we use encryption and other security technologies to safeguard your information.

5. Data Sharing

Internal Use: Your data may be shared internally within Mandaue Foam to improve app functionality and user experience.

Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services for app analytics and other purposes, but only with services that comply with our privacy standards.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your data if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. User Rights

As a user of the MF One app, you have the right to:

Access Your Data: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.

Correct Your Data: Request correction of any inaccuracies in your personal data.

Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain conditions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or other appropriate means.
